Research Interests

My research field mainly covers autonomous system, machine learning and robotics. Specifically, I am pretty interested in topics like 3D LiDAR SLAM, visual localization, place recognition, multi-robot system and other tasks for robotics such as perception, navigation and exploration. I am trying to apply vision and learning techniques to robotics so as to make robots more intelligent and smart in understanding the environment and performing accurate localization. Now I focus on multi-agent map merging that is totally automatic without human interaction and relative poses between trajectories.
