About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the CIS department of the University of Pennsylvania for my first year's study, advised by Prof. Mingmin Zhao. Before that, I received my M.S. from Tsinghua University in 2012 and B.Eng. from Tongji University in 2019.

The pronunciation of my name is /'haʊwən 'laɪ/. More information about me can be found in Bio and CV. If you are interested, feel free to contact me via email.

Research Interests

My research field mainly covers wireless sensing, autonomous system and robotics. Specifically, I am pretty interested in wireless perception for moving robots. Now I am focusing on imaging and reconstruction of the environment using wireless signals.
